Domain Formula Library

03

mps-formula is a standalone pure-formula library with 28 modules covering 300+ functions across physics, aerospace, and engineering domains. All formulas are pure Rust functions — no dependency on Rapier or WorldHandle.

🚀 Spaceflight (88 functions)

Orbital mechanics: Kepler period/semi-major-axis, elements↔state, J2/J3/J4 zonal harmonics, Gauss variational equations, Lambert universal solver, Hohmann transfer, Tsiolkovsky rocket equation, atmospheric drag/density, SRP, SGP4 secular rates, plane change ΔV.

Attitude/control/robotics: Quaternion derivative, Euler equations, CMG exchange/robust pseudoinverse, CW relative motion, TRIAD attitude, least-squares attitude, EKF scalar predict/gain/update, DH transform, manipulator dynamics, bang-off-bang profile, artificial potential guidance, docking glideslope, magnetic torquer.

Systems/environment: Solar panel power, thermal balance, CO₂ mass balance, Friis link, battery circuit, Hall thruster, flexible mode, slosh pendulum, heat pipe, radiator, Whipple shield, IGRF-13 tilted dipole, debris collision probability, atomic oxygen erosion.

🌌 Astrophysics (19)

N-body direct/Barnes-Hut/FMM, post-Newtonian correction, Roche limit, resonance detection, Lane-Emden, mass-luminosity, Eddington luminosity, Hubble law, NFW profile, Planck blackbody, Wien displacement, Jeans mass/length.

⚛️ Relativity (23)

Lorentz factor/boost, 4-vector transform, velocity addition, Schwarzschild metric, Kerr horizons/ergosphere/frame-dragging, ISCO (Schwarzschild+Kerr), gravitational redshift, Reissner-Nordstrom, light deflection, time dilation, length contraction.

🔬 Quantum (20)

Probability density, wave normalization, WKB transmission, barrier tunneling, zero-point energy, harmonic oscillator, free particle, de Broglie wavelength, infinite well, hydrogen atom (Bohr), uncertainty principle, Pauli matrices.

⚡ Electromagnetism (16)

🌊 Fluid (18)

AABB buoyancy/drag, simplified Navier-Stokes, SPH kernels (Poly6/Spiky/viscosity), SPH density/force estimation, Bernoulli, Reynolds number, Darcy friction factor, k-epsilon turbulence model.

🔥 Thermodynamics (23)

Fourier conduction, Stefan-Boltzmann radiation, phase change, FEM diffusion, ideal gas law, polytropic process, convective heat transfer, cycle efficiencies (Carnot/Otto/Diesel/Brayton), Clausius-Clapeyron, entropy.
