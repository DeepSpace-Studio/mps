地形引力

三种不规则地形引力计算方法：多面体 (Werner-Scheeres)、DEM (数字高程模型，直接求和或 FFT 加速)、月球 Mascon (12 个 GRAIL 质量瘤)。

多面体引力

Werner & Scheeres (1997) 算法 — 对任意多面体的精确引力解。适合不规则小行星、彗星。通过将小行星表面建模为三角面片集合，计算每个面片和每条边对引力势的解析贡献，精度仅受网格分辨率限制。

DEM 地形引力

将数字高程模型 (DEM) 转为表面质量分布，通过直接求和或 FFT 加速计算引力。支持任意分辨率。FFT 版本利用卷积定理，将 O(N²) 加速至 O(N log N)，适合高分辨率全球地形 (terrain_gravity_dem_fft)。

月球 Mascon 模型

12 个 GRAIL 任务导出的质量瘤 (Mass Concentration)。在 50 km 月轨高度产生 ~10⁻³ 到 10⁻⁵ m/s² 的额外引力——足以导致未建模时轨道坠毁。

内置 Mascon 列表：Imbrium, Serenitatis, Crisium, Nectaris, Humorum, Orientale, SPA, Smythii, Fecunditatis, Tranquillitatis, Procellarum, Frigoris。

Terrain Gravity

Three irregular terrain gravity methods: Polyhedron (Werner-Scheeres), DEM (Digital Elevation Model, direct sum or FFT-accelerated), Lunar Mascon (12 GRAIL mass concentrations).

Polyhedron Gravity

Werner & Scheeres (1997) algorithm — exact gravity solution for arbitrary polyhedra. Ideal for irregular asteroids and comets. Models the surface as a collection of triangular facets, computing analytic contributions from each face and edge to the gravitational potential — accuracy is limited only by mesh resolution.

DEM Terrain Gravity

Converts Digital Elevation Models into surface mass distributions. Direct summation or FFT-accelerated. Supports arbitrary resolution. The FFT variant leverages the convolution theorem for O(N log N) performance, suitable for high-resolution global terrain via terrain_gravity_dem_fft.

Lunar Mascon Model

12 GRAIL mass concentrations. At 50 km lunar altitude, produces ~10⁻³ to 10⁻⁵ m/s² additional gravity — enough to crash unmodeled orbits.

Built-in Mascons: Imbrium, Serenitatis, Crisium, Nectaris, Humorum, Orientale, SPA, Smythii, Fecunditatis, Tranquillitatis, Procellarum, Frigoris.
