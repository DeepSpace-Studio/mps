Werner & Scheeres (1997) 算法 — 对任意多面体的精确引力解。适合不规则小行星、彗星。

将数字高程模型 (DEM) 转为表面质量分布，直接求和或 FFT 加速。支持任意分辨率。

12 个 GRAIL 任务导出的质量瘤。在 50 km 月轨高度产生 ~10⁻³ 到 10⁻⁵ m/s² 的额外引力——足以导致未建模时轨道坠毁。

Werner & Scheeres (1997) algorithm — exact gravity solution for arbitrary polyhedra. Ideal for irregular asteroids and comets.

Converts Digital Elevation Models into surface mass distributions. Direct summation or FFT-accelerated. Supports arbitrary resolution.

12 GRAIL mass concentrations. At 50 km lunar altitude, produces ~10⁻³ to 10⁻⁵ m/s² additional gravity — enough to crash unmodeled orbits.
