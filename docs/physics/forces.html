Force Registry

ForceRegistry is a typed force dispatch system. Any force implementing ForceLaw trait is auto-scheduled during world_step with aggregated reporting via ForceFacade.

03

Built-in Force Laws

ForceLawTypeC SetterDescription
CelestialGravityworld_register_celestial_gravityAuto-selects harmonics/ellipsoid/J2 by altitude
NewtonianGravityworld_set_newton_gravity_lawO(n²) pairwise or Barnes-Hut (N>1000)
AirDragworld_set_air_drag_lawStokes + turbulent (Reynolds-aware)
CoulombFrictionworld_set_coulomb_friction_lawStatic + dynamic friction with velocity threshold
ExternalForceworld_set_external_force_lawBuoyancy, EM, spring, point gravity
